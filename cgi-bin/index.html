Company

The casery

Here at The Casery, we are a group of creative, energetic people inspired by the dream of bringing you artistically driven cell phone cases. Like our cases, everyone’s pretty unique here, and we have a lot of fun together.

We’re each an artist in our own right, whether that art is filmmaking, photography, or graphic design. That’s why the artists are our priority, and we aim to showcase their work in the best way possible. We believe that everyone deserves a cell phone case that they enjoy looking at all day, every day (because, let’s face it, you look at your cell phone more often than your girlfriend). And true art is the way to ensure that our cases offer more than just protection.

Our Mission

We live by the case, we die by the case. Inspiring creativity in a seriously fun way.

Our Dream

The Casery—powering the new wave of customization.

Core Values

Embrace Your Inner Artist

Some of us draw. Some paint. Others design. And some can barely hold a pencil. We have a case for everyone. Whether you want to design it from the ground up, start with a base design and make it your own, or have a talented artists design it for you, The Casery has you (and your phone) covered.

Quality First

The Casery believes in high quality business. Our products are designed to reach for perfection, in both artistic design and top grade materials.

Support the World

Our company is about expression, and we think the best way to get the word out is through The Casery. From supporting local artists, to offering non-profit designs, The Casery will use expression as a force for good.

Individuality

We like people to be different. We encourage our employees to be just as unique as our cases.

Creation That’s Fun

The retail industry is changing rapidly, but people will always follow the fun. We see the fun as the most important part of our business, and we’d like to keep it that way.

Stay Connected

We’re the Case Crew for a reason. We’re friends. And we’re friends with our artists and community, too. Keep us posted, and tell us what you’d like to see next.

about us

Case Crew

Matthew Harlow, CEO

Matthew founded The Casery in 2015 with the vision of creating a fully customizable, highly experiential marketplace. After graduating from the University of Southern California’s Marshall School of Business in 2009, Matthew founded a test prep and private tutoring company, in which he remains active. He has experience managing a Canadian-based SEO marketing company, as well as owning and operating a retail mail center. A natural competitor, Matthew made a name for himself at a young age by touring the world as a competitive yo-yoer. At the age of 16, Matthew became the World Yo-Yo Champion, in addition to two-time US National Champion and International Team Champion, where he served as team captain. He competed at a state level in billiards and ping pong, was second in the state in Foosball, and is a State Checkers Champion. He is artistically driven, and has been featured in the Phoenix Film Festival, has won several photography awards, and was a professional graphic design artist while attending New School for the Arts. His diverse experience in business, world-class competition, and the arts has helped him transform his vision for The Casery into a reality.

Skyler Milligan-LeCroy, Project Manager

Skyler graduated from the University of Southern California Marshall School of Business where he focused on project management, statistical analysis, and data research. He has extensive business experience working for LeCroy & Milligan Associates, a research and evaluation firm, where he successfully implemented a large-scale project management solution. His results exceeded expectations and pioneered faster turnaround times and a smoother throughput process for all future company endeavors. Skyler is also a champion public speaker and debater and was recognized as the Arizona State Champion of Policy Debate in 2012. He focuses his free time on a new health publication about public perceptions of child abuse. Skyler’s charisma, ability to work in a fast-paced team environment, and public speaking and debate skills make him an integral project manager of The Casery.

Marcus Fiebka, Creative Design Team Leader

Marcus is an artist and professional photographer with a background in banking. With an emphasis in financial analysis and evaluation, he currently holds his Series 6 and 63 licenses. Marcus’s background in both business and art qualifies him to lead the Creative Design Team and inspire his peers with an unrivaled sense of excitement.

contest

We are a young Southern California start-up company looking to expand our network and working relationship with the local, national and international art community. To achieve this goal, we are currently running a competition looking for the best and most creative designs suited for a cell phone case. Any medium will do and all our welcome to submit an unlimited number of designs. We ask that your designs follow the rules outlined in our terms and conditions but most importantly we want designs that you would enjoy seeing or having on your own phone case.

Submission must be:

- 6.5in. x 3.15in. (min. 600 dpi)

- 3900 x 1900 pixels minimum

- flattened psd, png, jpeg or gif

"THE CASERY" DESIGN CONTEST:

TERMS & CONDITIONS

1. Age Requirement This online competition is open to all artists 18 years of age or older.

2. Contest Duration This competition will begin on August 6, 2015, and will run until midnight on August 31, 2015.

3. Contest Participants The pool of potential participants will be drawn from various social media platforms (i.e Facebook, Twitter, Instagram and Snapchat); colleges around the Los Angeles Area; and Craigslist Posts. We are accepting up to 500 unique submissions.

4. Submission Requirements We are looking for colorful, modern, original artwork for our target demographic of 14 to 27 year olds. Prohibited Content: All submissions containing any of the following will be immediately disqualified and ineligible to win the contest: - Cursing - Offensive language/innuendos/scenarios - Death, blood, or violence - Gang related themes - Pornographic imagery

5. Work Must Be Original You must be the creator of the art that you submit to the competition. Your art must be your own original concept and not a copy of anyone else's copyrighted material. If your image infringes upon another person or entity's copyright(s), it will be immediately disqualified. Upon submitting your work to this competition, you are solely responsible for any infringement on copyrighted materials.

6. Extended Permission Required If the work of your submission is not solely yours, you must obtain and provide written permission from any owner of the work that you may submit the work to this contest and in compliance with all of the rules in these Terms and Conditions.

7. Entry Fees There is no entry fee or purchase necessary to enter this competition.

8. Repeat Entries Contestant are allowed to make multiple unique submissions to the current competition as long as the person submitting the entry has full legal ownership of the design.

9. Copyright Custom Vending Solutions, Inc. (dba "The Casery") will own all copyrights to all "winning" submission designs that are selected for production and produced for consumer sales by The Casery.

10. Choosing the Winner Up to 60 "winning designs" will be chosen from the submitted artwork. A pre-selected committee of four individuals will select the winning designs.

11. Payment Compensation All artists of "winning designs" will be compensated by check in accordance with the dollar amounts listed below. Winning artists will be compensated the agreed upon amount, regardless of how much or how little of their "winning design" is used in commerce.

1st place - USD $500.00

2nd-5th place - USD $200.00

6th-60th place - USD $50.00

12. Choice of Law Any disputes arising out of this agreement will be governed by the law of the state of California and shall be settled first by mediation, then if unsuccessful, by arbitration, in Los Angeles County, California.

13. Acknowledgement

By submitting an entry to this competition, contestant agrees to the terms and conditions above.
